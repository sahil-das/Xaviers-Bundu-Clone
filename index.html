Our History

St. Xavier’s High School Bundu has it’s beginning in January 1984 from class VI. It was a humble beginning. The people of Punch Purgana Region felt the need of a good school. At that time there was only S. S. High School Bundu. The First Batch wrote the Annual Board Examination in 1989. The Government had not yet given affiliation, so the students appeared for the exam from another schools till it got the Establishment Permission in March 23, 2008. The school got its permanent Recognition from HRD Ministry, Jharkhand Government in September 30, 2014. St. Xavier’s High School is owned and run by the Society of Jesus of Ranchi Province. The Society of Jesus, more commonly known as the Jesuits, is an International male Religious Congregation within the Roman Catholic Church that was founded by Ignatius of Loyola and instituted by Pope Paul III. Jesuits are well known in the field of education- founding School, Colleges, Universities etc. St. Xavier’s Bundu is run by Jesuits under the name of ‘Ranchi Jesuits’ a registered Society which is run by the Governing Body. The Governing Body has the sole authority in policy and decision making of the school. The School Managing Committee works under the Governing Body. The principal is responsible for looking after the day-to-day functioning of the school.
